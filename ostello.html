About Us

We are an online housing agency specializing in linking house owners, caretakers and agents with clients in need of accommodation within Lagos and also provide comprehensive help and support to our clients. Our major aim is to help our customers reduce the stress involved in looking for an apartment by understanding the type of accommodation needed, their available price range, desired location of the accomodation and liasing with agents. We also link private accommodation owner in need of a roommate meet nice and secured roommates.

Our Mission and Vision

Mission

To help reduce housing acquisition stress.

Vision

To become one of the biggest solution providers to the housing issues in Lagos.
